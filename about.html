Our heart

A prophetic ministry without borders

We're here to help you know God — to pray with confidence, hear His voice clearly, and be mentored in the Spirit. Whether you're a seasoned believer or simply curious, you'll find room to grow at your own pace.

Pray, meditate, and don't lose heart. Throughout the week we gather on simple online platforms, where people from all over the world show up, encounter God, and leave with testimonies.
