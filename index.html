Who we are

A prophetic ministry built for a connected world

We exist to help you love God, love people, and live your purpose. Our calling is simple: to equip anyone who wants to know God — to pray with confidence, hear His voice clearly, and grow with people who believe.

We're digital-first by design. Most of our family encounter God, find belonging, and even begin counselling without ever stepping into a building. And for those close by, our doors in Lagos are always open.
