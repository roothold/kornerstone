What it is

A one-on-one session, focused on you

Prophetic direction is a private appointment — you, and a prophet who has been trained to listen for God concerning your specific life, decisions, family, career, calling or ministry. It isn't a general prayer meeting. It's a focused, confidential space to receive what God is saying over the season you're in.

Some come with a big decision they need clarity on. Some come with a prophecy they've received and want to understand more deeply. Some come simply because they've been carrying something heavy for too long. All of it is welcome.

How it works

Three steps to your session

1

Choose in-person or online

If you're in Lagos, we ask you to come in person — the ministry is richer face-to-face. If you're outside Lagos, book online and we'll meet you on Zoom.

2

Pick a time and share your situation

Use the booking link below to choose an appointment slot that works for you. You'll be asked to briefly share what you're bringing to the session.

3

Come, listen, and receive

Your session is confidential and unhurried. Come with an open heart and expect God to meet you clearly and specifically.
