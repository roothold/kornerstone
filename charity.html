Charity & Outreach

Charity is about giving hope

An act of kindness through giving. We put love into action — meeting real needs in our communities, and inviting you to be part of it.

Home / Charity

Love, made practical

Meeting real needs, with dignity

At Kornerstone, we're passionate about meeting the needs of the less privileged in our society. Through feeding, education and outreach, we carry hope to people who need it most — and every act of kindness begins with someone willing to give.

Whether you give your time, your resources, or your prayers, you become part of how God reaches the vulnerable through us.

What we do

Hope in action

Academy

Investing in young lives through education — opening doors of opportunity for children who need them most.

Feeding Outreach

Hot meals and essentials for the hungry and vulnerable — served with warmth, dignity and love.

Relief & Support

Standing with families in hard seasons — practical relief and support for those facing real hardship.
