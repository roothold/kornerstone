How we started

From a summer session to a movement

Kornerstone Charity Initiative was founded from a deep compassion for vulnerable people and a commitment to creating lasting change within communities. What began as simple summer learning sessions for out-of-school children gradually evolved into a greater vision.

As we engaged with those children, we realised meaningful change required more than education. Every child is shaped by their family, environment, and the community around them — so our work grew to reach the full spectrum of human need: nourishment, dignity, rehabilitation and hope.

Today Kornerstone operates across three core arms, each distinct in focus yet united by one purpose: to love, serve, and empower people to thrive.

Our three arms

Distinct in focus, united in purpose

Kornerstone Education Initiative

We are passionate about ensuring no child is left behind because of a lack of access to quality education. Through structured learning programs, academic support and provision of educational resources, we walk alongside out-of-school and vulnerable children — equipping them with the knowledge and skills to build brighter futures and reach their full potential.

Kornerstone Feeding Program

We believe no one should face the day on an empty stomach. Our feeding program provides regular, nutritious meals to children and vulnerable individuals within our communities. Hunger is one of the greatest barriers to development — meeting this basic human need is the foundation upon which everything else is built.

Kornerstone Prison Outreach

Every person carries inherent dignity, regardless of circumstance. Our prison outreach arm extends compassion and practical support to incarcerated individuals — offering encouragement, rehabilitation resources, and a reminder that they are not forgotten. We walk with them on their journey toward restoration and meaningful reintegration.

What drives us

Two statements. One purpose.

Every meal served, every child taught, every prisoner remembered — traces back to the same reason we exist.

01
Mission

Holistic, compassionate support to vulnerable children and individuals — through education, nourishment and rehabilitation — empowering people at every stage of life to restore hope, recover dignity, and build a better future.

Made possible through the generous contributions of individuals and organisations committed to advancing human wellbeing and social transformation.

02
Vision

A future where every child can learn, grow and reach their full potential; every person has access to nourishment and care; and every individual, regardless of their past, is given the opportunity to be restored and reintegrated into a thriving, empowered community.
