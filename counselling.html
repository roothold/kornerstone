About our counselling

You don't have to figure it out alone

Whatever you're walking through, we're here for you. Our counselling offers Spirit-led guidance rooted in Scripture — in a space where you can be honest, supported, and never judged.

Faith-based & Spirit-led Strictly confidential A safe, judgement-free space Members & non-members In person & online
Types of counselling

Find the right kind of support

Individual Counselling

Personal, one-to-one support for life's challenges — emotional, spiritual and practical — at your own pace.

Marriage Counselling

Guidance for couples seeking to strengthen, restore or deepen their union on a godly foundation.

Pre-Marital Counselling

Preparation for couples approaching marriage — building communication, expectations and shared purpose.

Leadership & Ministry Counselling

Support for leaders and ministers navigating the unique pressures of calling, service and influence.

Confidentiality & ethics

What you share stays safe

Our promise to you

All counselling sessions are strictly confidential. What you share is never disclosed to anyone without your explicit consent. We hold your trust with the seriousness it deserves, in line with biblical and professional ethical standards.
